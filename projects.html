Lightning Blue Lizards (LBL)

Lightning Blue Lizards (LBL) Is a First Robotics Competition team from The Columbus School. LBL started in 2019 and I was part of the first generation that traveled to compete in the South Florida Regional where we achieved the recognition as best rookie team and therefore passed to the World Championship in Houston Texas. Click here to read more about the competition


We had to develop a manually operated robot that was able to achieve certain task such as gathering balls from the ground and throwing them into elevated holes to achieve points. To achieve this, we programmed the robot in Java using Phoenix 5 and Grapple Robotics libraries. Also, we had a whole team in charge of the mechanic aspects of the robot.


I was part of the programming team. Specifically, I was in charge of programming the controls for the robot. But also, I help in the mechanic construction and assemble.

Angkor

Angkor is a heavy metal band formed in 2003 in Medellin with the help and promotion of the band, Kraken.


I became the lead guitarist in 2021. Since then, we have recorded an album and played live. This became my first serious band where I learned many skills that I still apply to this day such as recording, playing live, music production and distribution. Watch Angkor Live Performance


For recording our album, we worked with a producer in Medellin that specialized in rock bands. For recording the guitar parts, we used Guitar Rig 5, which is a VST Plugin for a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW). And for the DAW we used Pro Tools that is popular for modern music production. Mordred Damon

Mordred Damon is my solo music project. I started in 2018 when I started composing my own songs for electric guitar and uploading them to music platforms and social media. Although is a small personal project, it was my first approach to the music industry, and it allowed me to present and engage with other artists and allowing me to start working as a musician. Listen to my songs on Spotify


My first recordings were done in my house using an Icon Ultra 4 Audio Interface and recording into Cakewalk BandLab (DAW). To upload my songs into music platforms, I used DistroKid which is an online music distribution service.
